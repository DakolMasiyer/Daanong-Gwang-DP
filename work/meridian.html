A feature drama set in the semi-arid interior of northern Nigeria, Meridian follows a government surveyor whose mapping mission unravels into a confrontation with the communities he has been sent to assess.

The cinematography uses the landscape as dramatic counterpoint — the openness of the terrain in stark contrast to the psychological claustrophobia of the narrative. Hard daylight and practical night sources dominate the palette.
