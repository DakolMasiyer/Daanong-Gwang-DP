A PrEP HIV prevention awareness film for the MTV Shuga campaign — educating young Africans about Pre-Exposure Prophylaxis, normalising HIV prevention conversations, and reducing stigma around sexual health.

Part of the wider MTV Staying Alive Foundation's edutainment strategy, which has reached hundreds of millions of young people across Africa through film, television, and digital content.
