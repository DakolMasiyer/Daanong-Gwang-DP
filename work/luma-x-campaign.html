A high-end commercial campaign for Luma X, a technology brand launching a new product line across Africa and Europe. The brief required a visual language that was minimal, aspirational, and technically precise.

The shoot was predominantly studio-based, with a controlled lighting setup built to allow maximum colour accuracy and editorial flexibility in post. The grade was developed in close collaboration with the colourist over three passes.
