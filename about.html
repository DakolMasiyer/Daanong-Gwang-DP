Biography

Da'anong Gyang is a Director of Photography working across documentary, feature film, and commercial production. Based between Lagos and London, he brings a deeply observational eye to every project — drawn to natural light, landscape, and the quiet power of the unguarded moment.

His documentary work has screened at festivals across West Africa, Europe, and North America. In narrative film, he collaborates closely with directors to develop visual languages that serve story above spectacle. His commercial work is sought for its ability to carry the same cinematic weight into short-form content.

Before moving behind the camera full-time, Da'anong studied fine art in Jos and cinematography at the London Film School. He continues to shoot on film alongside digital production.
