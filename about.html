Biography

Da'anong Gyang is a Director of Photography whose work blends technical precision with emotionally driven visual storytelling. With close to a decade of experience, he has built a reputation for cinematic images that enhance narrative depth and resonate with audiences across film and television. His work has screened internationally, including Leading Lady at the Motion Picture International Film Festival and the Toronto International Nollywood Film Festival.

His cinematography credits span film and television, including projects for Amazon Prime, M-Net, Showmax South Africa, and MTV Shuga Naija — among them Halita, Agu, The Real Housewives of Abuja, and Bae Beyond Borders: South Africa. His recent film Finding Nina won Best Picture at the Arewa Film Festival and earned nominations for Best Cinematography at the Zuma Film Festival and the Best of Nollywood Awards.

ARRI-certified in camera systems and Steadicam operation, Da'anong brings technical mastery and narrative sensitivity to visuals that elevate stories and connect with audiences.
